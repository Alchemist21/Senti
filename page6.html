      Stroke Survivors on Twitter: Sentiment and Topic Analysis From a Gender Perspective

      Sentiment analysis based on National Research Council R package. The emotion scores for men and women were first subjected to an F-test and then to a Wilcoxon rank sum test.

      Study found out that women express positive emotions and happiness much more than men.


    Rudolph AG et al. Stroke Survivors on Twitter: Sentiment and Topic Analysis from a Gender Perspective. J Med Internet Res. 2019 Aug26;21(8).
